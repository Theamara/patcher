WeatherFresnelQuad (Skyrim)
Summary
Represents a set of weather fresnel power for each of the four parts of the day: sunrise, day, sunset and night.
Properties
Name Description
float Day Gets or sets the weather fresnel power during the day.
float Night Gets or sets the weather fresnel power during the night.
float Sunrise Gets or sets the weather fresnel power during the sunrise.
float Sunset Gets or sets the weather fresnel power during the sunset.
