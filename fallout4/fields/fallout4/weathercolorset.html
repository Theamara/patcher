WeatherColorSet (Fallout4)
Summary
Represents a set of weather colors for each of the eight parts of the day: early dawn, dawn, late dawn, day, early dusk, late dusk and night.
Properties
Name Description
Color Dawn Gets or sets the weather color during the early dawn.
Color Day Gets or sets the weather color during the day.
Color Dusk Gets or sets the weather color during the dusk.
Color EarlyDawn Gets or sets the weather color during the dawn.
Color EarlyDusk Gets or sets the weather color during the early dusk.
Color LateDawn Gets or sets the weather color during the late dawn.
Color LateDusk Gets or sets the weather color during the late dusk.
Color Night Gets or sets the weather color during the night.
